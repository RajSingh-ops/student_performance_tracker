About YearScore

A simple way to track your progress, honestly.

YearScore was created as a personal project to solve a simple problem — keeping track of growth without overcomplicating things.

Most productivity tools try to do too much. This one focuses on what actually matters: consistency, reflection, and visible progress over time.

Whether it’s daily habits, achievements, or personal goals, YearScore is meant to stay out of your way and let you focus on the work.

Why this exists

This platform started as a learning project during my engineering journey. Over time, it became something useful enough to share.

It’s still evolving. Some features are simple, some are experimental, and some ideas are still in progress.

The goal

The goal isn’t perfection. It’s progress you can look back on and understand.

If this helps you stay consistent even a little — then it’s doing its job.
